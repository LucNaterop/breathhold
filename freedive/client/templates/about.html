About This App

CO2 Tolerance Table

This table increases your body’s acceptance to higher levels of Carbon dioxide (CO2) in your blood. A certain CO2 level in your blood triggers your breath. The higher the CO2 tolerance the lower your urge to breathe. The values of this table can be changed via your Maximum Breath Hold Time.


O2 Deprivation Table

This table helps you to deal with low levels of Oxygen (O2) in your blood. Holding your breath reduces the O2 level in your blood. It is imperative to train your body to low oxygen consumption when holding your breath. The values of this table can be changed via your Maximum Breath Hold Time


Adjustment of the Tables

The O2 Deprivation table and the CO2 Tolerance table can be adjusted to your personal results.

  • Access the MENU
  • Select SETTINGS
  • Change the Maximum Breath Hold Time via the up and down arrows or via the slider


Personal Table

In order to create your personalized table:

  • Access the screen TABLES
  • Tap on the white “+” sign on the top right corner
  • Change the Maximum Breath Hold Time via the up and down arrows or via the slider
